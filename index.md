# Spark FIRE — Privacy Policy
**Last updated: March 2026**

## No Data Collected
Spark FIRE does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal 
or financial information. Your data never leaves your device.

## What Stays on Your Device
All information you enter — income, expenses, net worth, age, and 
retirement projections — is stored locally on your iPhone using 
Apple's on-device storage (SwiftData). It is never uploaded, synced, 
or accessible to anyone other than you.

## No Analytics or Tracking
Spark FIRE contains no analytics SDKs, no crash reporting services, 
no advertising networks, and no third-party code that accesses your 
data. The app makes zero network requests.

## No Account Required
Spark FIRE does not require you to create an account, provide an 
email address, or authenticate with any service.

## In-App Purchases
Spark FIRE uses Apple's StoreKit for the optional Pro upgrade. 
Purchase transactions are handled entirely by Apple. Spark FIRE 
does not process or store any payment information.

## Widgets
The home screen widget displays data from your on-device profile 
using Apple's App Group mechanism. No data leaves your device.

## Children's Privacy
Spark FIRE does not knowingly collect information from anyone, 
including children under 13.

## Educational Disclaimer
Spark FIRE is for educational and informational purposes only. 
It does not provide financial, investment, tax, or legal advice. 
Always consult a qualified financial advisor before making 
financial decisions.
